What is THE COVENANT

A world built in darkness. A hierarchy sealed in blood.

THE COVENANT is a 150-piece occult NFT collection rooted in black magic, voodoo, and the mythology of power. Five bloodlines. Fourteen characters. One legendary piece that belongs to no bloodline — it belongs to no one, until it does.

Each piece carries a bloodline. Each bloodline carries a curse. The rarer the piece, the deeper the covenant runs.
