The Origin
The Crossroads

The crossroads appears at the end of a road you have walked a hundred times. Five paths where there used to be one. He is already there, which should not surprise you.

Tall hat. Bone face. A cigar that has no interest in going out. He is laughing at something when you arrive. You never find out what.

Here is what nobody tells you about him. He could send you home. He has that power. He can cure anything, lift any curse, decide someone is not worth the ink and turn them away whole. He does it sometimes, when the want in someone does not interest him enough to write their name down.

Yours interests him.

Five bloodlines have been written at this crossing. Five different prices for five different wants. The deal does not announce itself. There is no ceremony. You go home, you sleep, you wake up and something is slightly different. Then something else. Then something else again. You tell yourself you are adjusting. Some of them never stop telling themselves that.

He digs the graves for what you were before you came here. Considers it part of the job. He is thorough about it.

He keeps the ledger. Every name, every want, every cost. He knows what you are giving up before you do. He could tell you. He will not.

He is not the villain of this story.

That is the part that should worry you.
