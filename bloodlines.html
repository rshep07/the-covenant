The Bloodlines

Five deals made at the same crossing. Five different prices. Five different ways the deal gets into your blood and does its work.

01 / 05
Crimson

He gave them power over flesh. The kind that lets you take a life, keep one going, or do things to a body that no prayer, charm, or locked door can protect against. He marks it all down. Every death. Every life spared. All of it his.

What they lose is harder to see. The hesitation. The mercy. The part of them that used to feel bad about it.

02 / 05
Mystic

The Mystic bloodline wanted what was locked away on purpose. Not rumors. Not secrets passed around by frightened people. The stuff that was hidden because knowing it changes you in ways you cannot come back from.

He gave them the key, and what he gets back is everything they find. He knows what they know before they have time to understand what it means.

03 / 05
Spirit

He governs the dead, but the dead do not stay neatly where they are put. They wander. They cling. They get lost in doorways, bedrooms, mirrors, and places where grief has worn a path into the floor.

So he made a deal. The price is belonging. They live between two worlds and are claimed by neither. The living feel something wrong before they can name it. The dead know exactly what they are.

04 / 05
Wild

The Wild bloodline wanted to stop being human. Not to die. To be done with the weakness of being one person in one body for one short life.

He rules what the ground receives and what it gives back. He let them become part of that cycle. It starts in the skin and works inward. Quietly. Most do not notice what is missing until they reach for it and find nothing there.

05 / 05
Cursed

The Cursed bloodline never made a deal. Someone before them did. The terms were broken. The debt went unpaid. He does not forgive debts. He passes them down.

They were born already owing. He could end it. He just has not decided they are worth the answer.
